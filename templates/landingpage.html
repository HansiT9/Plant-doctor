About Us

Welcome to Tea Leaves Disease Prediction, an innovative web application designed to help tea farmers and enthusiasts prevent and manage tea plant diseases.

Our goal is to provide a simple, user-friendly tool that allows users to quickly and accurately identify common tea Leaves diseases and take appropriate action to protect their crops.

Our team is comprised of experts in plant pathology, machine learning. We have collaborated to create an advanced algorithm that can analyze images of tea leaves and accurately predict the presence of various diseases, including black rot, blister blight, and grey blight add others too.

Our web application is easy to use and accessible from any device with an internet connection. Simply upload an image of your tea leaves and our algorithm will analyze it to determine the likelihood of disease. We also provide information on how to prevent and treat the disease to help you maintain healthy tea plants and maximize your crop yield.

Thank you for choosing Tea Leaves Disease Prediction as your go-to resource for tea plant health. We hope our web application helps you maintain healthy and productive tea plants.





Our Story

Our Dr. Tea tealeaves disease prediction web was created with one goal in mind: to help tea farmers protect their crops from diseases and increase their yields. As farmers ourselves, we understand the challenges and risks associated with crop management, and we wanted to develop a web that could help farmers overcome these challenges.

Our team consists of experienced tea farmers, agricultural experts, and software developers who worked together to create an innovative tealeaves disease prediction web application. We combined our knowledge of tea farming with the latest technologies in machine learning and data analysis to create a tool that is accurate, reliable, and easy to use.

Our Dr. Tea web application uses advanced algorithms to analyze environmental data, weather patterns, and other factors to predict potential diseases in tealeaves. The app provides farmers with real-time alerts and recommendations on preventive measures, helping them to take action quickly and minimize crop loss.

We believe that our tealeaves disease prediction web app can revolutionize the way tea farmers manage their crops, and we are committed to making it accessible to farmers around the world. Our goal is to empower farmers with the knowledge and tools they need to protect their crops and increase their yields, while also promoting sustainable and environmentally friendly farming practices.

Join us on our mission to help tea farmers succeed, one tealeaf at a time.
